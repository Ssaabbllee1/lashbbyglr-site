SABLE GRAYSON is a Bella Lash certified esthetician and last artist specializing in eyelash extensions since 2019. Since, she has developed a luxurious, high-end approach to amplifying your natural beauty offering luxe solutions and services.

LASHBBYGRL was formed in 2021 as a mobile esthetician service providing in-home solutions to lash extensions, lifts and tints, waxing, brow laminations and tooth gems.

As a mobile artist, LASHBBYGRL and our mobile studio setup brings masterful esthetician solutions to your home, maximizing your comfort and prioritizing clientele preference while providing the credibility and satisfaction of an in-studio beauty bar, offering professional beauty services and products.

Sable is a licensed Esthetician, a Cancer sun, Virgo moon, and Aries rising. She has three cats: Sniffles, Sassy and Sasha, and an appreciation of all things esthetics.
