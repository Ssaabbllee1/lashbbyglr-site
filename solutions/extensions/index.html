LASH EXTENSIONS

Classic extensions are the most natural set offered. One lash extension is placed on one natural lash. A classic set is perfect for clients who already have a lot of lashes, but are looking to add length.

Hybrid extensions are a mix between classic and volume extensions. They give a more full effect with volume and length, applying a wispy look to your lashes.

Volume extensions are the most dramatic set, where multiplied lash extensions are fanned out and then applied to one natural lash. All fans are handmade and custom tailored.
